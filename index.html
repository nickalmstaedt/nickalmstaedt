Profile

Where the briefing room meets the battlefield.

I am an international security professional and German Bundeswehr officer with over two decades of operational leadership across NATO, UN, and bilateral missions — from Afghanistan and Iraq to Mali, South Sudan, Djibouti, and the Western Balkans.

Currently serving as DCB Crisis Management Initiative Leader for Bosnia and Herzegovina at NATO HQ International Staff in Sarajevo, I translate complex geopolitical realities into actionable strategy for defence organisations, policy institutions, and private sector clients. My current analytical focus is on strategic foresight, early warning, and creeping-crisis dynamics in fragile security environments. I have been working as an independent adviser on selected mandates since 2014.

Under the label Middle East Minds, I develop bespoke analyses and facilitate structured engagements on conflict dynamics, governance fragility, and strategic competition across the Middle East and Africa — available on request since 2023.
