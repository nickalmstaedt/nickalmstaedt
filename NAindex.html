Profile

Where the briefing room meets the battlefield.

I am a German Bundeswehr reserve officer and international security professional with over two decades of operational leadership across NATO, UN, and bilateral missions — from Afghanistan and Iraq to Mali, South Sudan, and the Western Balkans.

Currently serving as DCB Crisis Management Initiative Leader for Bosnia and Herzegovina at NATO HQ International Staff in Sarajevo, I translate complex geopolitical realities into actionable strategy for defence organisations, policy institutions, and private sector clients.

My analytical work is published under the label Middle East Minds — tracking conflict dynamics, governance fragility, and strategic competition across the Middle East and Africa since 2014.
